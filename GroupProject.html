Protect your home against it's biggest threat...water.

  1. Painted surfaces will wear significantly faster when wet, causing dmage such as peeling, chipping and potential rotting sooner than normal
  2. Water by a cement pad will undermine the pad and cause frost heaves that will crack the pad. This leads to flooded basements and other damage to your home.
  3. Water will undermine any stone walls, walkways or stonework.
  4. Water spalshing by houses will rot any exposed wood, leading to replacing wood siding, decking etc.
  5. Splashing water will ruin any plantings or landscape by the house.
